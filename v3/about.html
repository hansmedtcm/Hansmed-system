Our origin 緣起

Why we built HansMed 為何創辦 HansMed

HansMed was born from a simple idea — to help busy Malaysians see a qualified TCM practitioner with peace of mind, while freeing doctors from paperwork so they can focus on what they trained for: listening, feeling the pulse, understanding your constitution. 為了方便大眾在忙碌的生活節奏中可以安心看上合格中醫，讓中醫師專心做他們一輩子在做的事 —— 聽您說，把您的脈，了解您的體質。

Modern technology, traditional wisdom — bridging the generations. 現代化的科技平台，傳統式的中醫智慧 — 造就了現代與傳統不再有代溝。

Our practice 診室

A space for quiet listening 為您 專注傾聽

Our practitioners work in a calm, traditional setting designed for the time-honored craft of TCM diagnosis — pulse, tongue, voice, observation. The technology is invisible; what you experience is care. 我們的中醫師在一個寧靜、傳統的環境中工作，專為望聞問切而設。科技在背後運作，您所體驗的是照護本身。

Our approach 原則

Three things we never compromise on 我們絕不妥協的 三件事

Clinical integrity 臨床誠信

Every AI output and wellness report is reviewed by a licensed TCM practitioner before it reaches you. All practitioners are registered with Malaysia's T&CM Council and undergo continuous education. 所有 AI 健康報告均由持牌中醫師審核後才呈現給您。所有醫師均於馬來西亞傳統與輔助醫藥局註冊，並持續進修中醫學識。

Privacy first 私隱優先

Fully PDPA 2010 compliant. Explicit consent before any AI processing. Encrypted storage — export or delete your data at any time from settings. 完全符合 2010 年個人資料保護法（PDPA）。AI 處理前必須取得明確同意。資料加密儲存 — 您可隨時於設定中匯出或刪除個人資料。

Quality herbs 品質藥材

Only verified Malaysian and international suppliers. Every product labelled with Chinese name, pinyin, and transparent sourcing details. 只與經認證的本地及國際供應商合作。每款藥材均附上中文名稱、拼音及完整的來源資訊，公開透明。
