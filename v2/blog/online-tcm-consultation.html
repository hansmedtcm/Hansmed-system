The Pros of Online TCM Consultation

線上中醫問診之優勢

— And When to Come In Person

— 及何時需到診

Mr. Lim Gao Hong · February 2026 · 5 min read

Video consultations are not a compromise — for many conditions, they are equivalent in outcome to in-person visits.

What works well online

Constitution assessment, follow-ups, lifestyle coaching, herbal prescription renewals, menstrual consultations, sleep and digestion issues.

When in-person is better

Pulse diagnosis at first visit, acupuncture, tui na, cupping, and any acute musculoskeletal issue that needs hands-on assessment.

Our hybrid model

HansMed practitioners offer both — the first consult can be online, and you only come to the clinic if your practitioner decides a physical touch point is needed.
