The Ancient Art of Tongue Reading

Mr. Siew Kuen Xian · January 2026 · 5 min read

Your tongue is a map of your internal health. Colour, coating, shape, and moisture reveal information your symptoms alone cannot.

Colour

Pale points to blood or yang deficiency; bright red to heat; purple to stasis.

Coating

Thick white indicates cold or damp; yellow suggests heat; no coating at all can signal yin deficiency.

Shape

Swollen with teeth-marks suggests spleen qi weakness; thin and small points to yin or blood deficiency.

舌象為內臟之鏡。顏色、苔、形狀及潤度，皆透露症狀以外之訊息。

顏色

淡白 — 血虛或陽虛；紅絳 — 熱證；紫暗 — 瘀血。

苔

白厚 — 寒或濕；黃 — 熱；無苔 — 陰虛。

形狀

胖大齒痕 — 脾氣虛；瘦小 — 陰虛或血虛。
