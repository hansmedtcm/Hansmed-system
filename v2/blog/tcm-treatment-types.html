Understanding TCM Treatment Types

認識中醫療法

Acupuncture, Herbal Medicine & Manipulation

針灸、中藥與推拿

Mr. Siew Kuen Xian · March 2026 · 5 min read

Traditional Chinese Medicine is not a single therapy — it is a system built from three core modalities that work together.

Acupuncture · 針灸

Fine needles inserted at specific points to regulate qi flow, relieve pain, and balance organ systems. Best for musculoskeletal pain, stress, migraines, and post-operative recovery.

Herbal Medicine · 中藥

Formulas drawn from over 400 herbs, tailored to each patient is constitution. Used for internal conditions: digestion, sleep, menstrual health, immune support.

Manipulation · 推拿

Tui na (massage) and cupping work the meridians externally. Often combined with acupuncture for muscular and circulatory issues.

中醫並非單一療法，而是由三大核心療法相輔相成的系統。

針灸 · Acupuncture

於特定穴位施以細針，調節氣之運行，舒緩疼痛，平衡臟腑。適用於肌肉骨骼疼痛、壓力、偏頭痛及術後恢復。

中藥 · Herbal Medicine

由逾400味中藥組成之方劑，因人制宜。主治內科：消化、睡眠、婦科、免疫。

推拿 · Manipulation

推拿與拔罐從外部作用於經絡，常與針灸合併用於肌肉及循環問題。
