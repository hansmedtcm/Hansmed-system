# Compliance Copy Changes — HansMed TCM Platform

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-04-21
**Owner:** Compliance (coordinating Claude)
**Applies to:** Frontend v2/ strings, backend API response messages, email templates, PDF outputs

## Purpose

Bring all user-facing copy into alignment with:
1. **Malaysia Medical Device Act 2012** — unlicensed software cannot present output as "diagnosis"
2. **T&CM Act 2016** — only registered practitioners may issue clinical recommendations
3. **PDPA 2010** — consent and processing notices must be explicit
4. **Claude Usage Policy** — AI output framed as educational/wellness, not medical diagnosis

## Required Disclaimers (must appear)

### D1 — AI Wellness Insight pages

> This wellness analysis is generated by software for educational purposes only. It is not a medical diagnosis, does not replace consultation with a registered healthcare practitioner, and should not be used to make treatment decisions. If you have symptoms that concern you, please consult a qualified practitioner or visit a clinic.

Placement: directly above any AI-generated output block; minimum 14px body text; must not be collapsed/hidden by default.

### D2 — Tongue Wellness Scan upload screen

> You are about to upload a photograph of your tongue for analysis. By continuing, you consent to image processing under our Privacy Notice. Images are stored encrypted and may be retained for up to 90 days for service improvement unless you request earlier deletion. This scan provides wellness insights only and is not a clinical assessment.

### D3 — Questionnaire screen

> Your answers help our system generate a general wellness profile. They are not a substitute for consultation with a registered practitioner. Answers are stored confidentially and handled under our Privacy Notice.

### D4 — Practitioner recommendation PDF footer

> Issued by [Practitioner Name], T&CM Council Registration No. [XXXX]. Valid for [N] days from issue date. Not transferable.

### D5 — Footer of every page

> HansMed is a wellness technology platform. Clinical services on this platform are provided by independently registered T&CM practitioners. HansMed does not provide medical advice.
